## ✨ Features

- 📰 **News feed** — browse and read student-submitted articles
- 🖼️ **Image upload** — attach images to news posts
- 🔐 **User authentication** — register, log in and manage your posts
- 🗂️ **News categories** — filter articles by topic or department
